Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Keep ("we", "our", or "us") is a dice game scoreboard app. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use Keep:

  • Anonymous user ID — automatically assigned when you open the app. No name, email, or personal details are required.
  • Game data — player names you enter, scores, and game history you choose to save. This is stored in your account.
  • Purchase information — if you upgrade to premium, RevenueCat processes and manages your subscription or purchase. We do not store your payment details.
  • Ad data — Google AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve ads to free-tier users. See Google's privacy policy for details.
  • Analytics — basic usage analytics via Firebase to help us understand how the app is used.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To save your game history and player groups across sessions
  • To manage your premium entitlement and restore purchases
  • To serve ads to free-tier users
  • To improve the app based on usage patterns

3. Third-Party Services

Keep uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase — authentication and data storage
  • RevenueCat — in-app purchase management
  • Google AdMob — advertising for free-tier users

4. Data Retention

Your game data is retained as long as your account exists. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

5. Children's Privacy

Keep is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

6. Your Choices

  • You can use Keep without providing any personal information
  • You can request deletion of your data by contacting us
  • You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We'll update the date at the top of this page when we do.
